Building a cognitive model.

Step One - Make sure you are familiar with the system

Think about the nature of the system you are trying to simulate.  Note all components in the system and how they interact with each other (Click here for a diagram of the fallow system).

Step Two - Define the relationships.

Define the nature of ALL relationships between the components of your system and put some sort of weighting on the importance of each. Ensure that your particular area of interest does not automatically take centre stage!

You now have a simple model of what you percieve to be happening in the system being studied from which you can deduce the main areas of concern for parameterisation.
