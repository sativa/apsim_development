Soil water balance

The self calculated water balance.

The water balance equation here is

Change in Storage = Rain + Irrigation - Evaporation - Runoff - Drainage

or

Losses = Evaporation + Runoff + Drainage= Rain + Irrigation - Change in Storage

It should be possible to check all fallow periods for agreement between observed soil water data and observed inputs and losses.  For example

  • If the observed data suggest zero or minimal drainage and there were no rainfall events for which runoff would have occurred then you can test that the following calculation gives sensible estimates.
    Evaporation  = Rain + Irrigation - Change in Storage

The underlying question is Do you believe your current input data? The model will ensure a mass balance.  If your data does not allow a mass balance then the model will never simulate your data well.
