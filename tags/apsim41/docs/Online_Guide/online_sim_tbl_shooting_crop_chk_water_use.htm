Crop growth & development

Checking overall water usage

In situations where measured data is minimal measurements of pre and post crop soil resources are all that exist.  In these situations several aspects of the simulation can be checked.

  1. Check the initialisation values for soil moisture (Check these in the summary file - not the parameter file).  There is little sense in comparing later observations if the initial values are incorrect.

  2. The plant available water range. In many cases the post harvest measured water content will be very close to the crop lower limit for most surface layers.  The lower layers may only approach the crop lower limit if a significant crop has been grown and the crop's root system has been able to follow this resource down through the profile.

  3. The level of usage of the water resource.  The incorrect level of water use by the model may be due to either error in the calculation of loss mechanisms (see the section on water balance budgeting form more information) altering the calculation of water supply OR due to a parallel restriction on crop growth reducing the demand for soil water(see the section on determining limiting resources form more information).
