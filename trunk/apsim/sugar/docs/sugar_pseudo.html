Sugar Nfact


The concentration of Nitrogen in leaves is used to derive a series of Nitrogen stress indices. The stress indices for photosynthesis and cell expansion are calculated from today's relative nutritional status between a critical and minimum leaf Nitrogen concentration.

Subprogram Logic


n_conc_leaf = divide (g_n_green leaf, g_dm_green leaf, 0 . 0)
n_leaf_crit = g_n_conc_crit leaf * g_dm_green leaf
n_conc_leaf_crit = divide (n_leaf_crit, g_dm_green leaf, 0 . 0)
n_leaf_min = g_n_conc_min leaf * g_dm_green leaf
n_conc_leaf_min = divide (n_leaf_min, g_dm_green leaf, 0 . 0)
n_conc_ratio = divide ((n_conc_leaf-n_conc_leaf_min), (n_conc_leaf_crit-n_conc_leaf_min), 0 . 0)
n_def = k_nfact * n_conc_ratio
nfact = bound (n_def, 0 . 0, 1 . 0)
