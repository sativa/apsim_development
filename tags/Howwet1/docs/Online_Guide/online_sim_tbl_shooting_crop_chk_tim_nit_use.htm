Crop growth & development

Checking timing of nitrogen usage

The main factors affecting the rate of nitrogen usage are as follows:

  1. Nitrogen demand. Error in nitrogen uptake could be either the result or cause of errors in the timecourse of nitrogen uptake. (A potential growth simulation may help rule either out)

  2. Time in a layer containing nitrogen. This is mostly determined by root front advance rates. Check the timing of water usage

  3. The extraction of water from a layer. This is determined by root front advance rates and crop water demands. Check the timing of water usage

  4. Nitrogen Content of the soil explored by roots through time.  Check the observed soil data (including replicates) if the nitrogen balance varies widely from that observed in the field.  Do the observed data allow for a Nitrogen Balance?
