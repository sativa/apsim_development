Crop growth & development

Absence of nitrogen information.

The absence of Nitrogen resource information can significantly limit informed adjustment of simulation configuration.  Some estimate of soil nitrogen resource can be derived from simple crop data such as grain nitrogen concentration (See Estimating Crop Nitrogen Requirement).

Using this information the user can compare the crop management information (e.g. fertiliser application) and soil specification (e.g. fertility status) to determine whether the current configuration would supply adequate nitrogen for the observed crop yield.  If there is some disparity then either assumptions regarding the soil fertility, or information regarding initial conditions or management may need reviewing.
