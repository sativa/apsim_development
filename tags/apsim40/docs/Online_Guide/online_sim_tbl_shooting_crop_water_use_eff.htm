Crop water use efficiency

Estimates of crop water use can be calculated from final yield* or biomass* and approximate crop Water Use Efficiency (WUE).  WUE has units of weight or plant matter per unit area per unit of crop water use (e.g. kg/ha/mm).  Please make note of the exact units described in the table for the species of interest.
