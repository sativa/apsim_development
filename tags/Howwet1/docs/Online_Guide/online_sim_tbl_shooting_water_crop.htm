Trouble shooting crop water balance.

The idea of trouble shooting crop water balance prediction errors in isolation of the predictive capability of the rest of the crop growth calculations is fraught with danger.   As a result, an approach that takes each aspect of crop growth into account is most suitable, even if the water balance is the main area of concern.
