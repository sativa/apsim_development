Soil nitrogen balance

Hand calculations of nitrogen balances.

For Fallows

  1. Calculate net mineralisation via soil nitrogen content balances during each fallow and study any differences.  Can you explain differences between seasons?  Crop interactions?  Sample error?

  2. Calclate nitrogen balances for sections of the profile?  Is there evidence of leaching?  Is there evidence of deep crop uptake prior to the fallow?  Do the numbers balance through time?

For Crops

  1. Determine the crop Nitrogen balance - ie Nitrogen uptake or requirement.  It is worth testing that the observed data agrees with estimates of nitrogen uptake requirements for each crop.   The model maintains a Nitrogen Mass Balance and any data that does not exhibit a mass balance will never be simulated well.  This will require estimates of soil mineralisation.

  2. Estimates of in-crop mineralisation rates - The amount of N mineralised during a crop can sometimes be estimated from observed N uptake data, especially in cases of zero fertiliser application.  The assumption here is that the crop acts as a constant sampling mechanism and thus total plant Nitrogen through time is approximately equal to the soil mineralisation rate.
