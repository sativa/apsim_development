Crop growth & development

Step Three - Checking Crop Resource Use

Most divergence in crop simulation results can be found to be due to incorrect resource use or supply.

Once again level of detail of measured water and nitrogen supply will necessitate different levels of comparison.  The important tasks of this comparison, however, are the same.

  1. To check the overall usage of any resource.

  2. To check the timing of use of any resource throughout the season (this is important for feedback mechanisms)

  3. To check that a complete budget of resource can account for all additions or losses of resource.
