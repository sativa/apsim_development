Crop harvest index

The Harvest Index (HI) can be used to convert either grain yield to final biomass or vice versa.  The value describes the fraction of above-ground biomass in the yield component at final harvest.  The following table lists the harvest indices for several major crop species.  For severe stresses around flowering or late stress then HI would tend toward the lower end of the range.  In average conditions HI would tend toward the middle of the range.

Crop Species

Harvest Index

Wheat

0.4-0.45

Sorghum

0.4-0.45

Maize

0.4-0.45

Sugar Cane

0.7 (cane dry weight/total)

Chickpea

0.35-0.45

Mungbean

0.35-0.45

Sunflower

0.35-0.45

Peanut

0.35-0.45

Soybean

0.35-0.45
