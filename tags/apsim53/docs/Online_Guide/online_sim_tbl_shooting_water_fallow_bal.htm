Soil water balance

Trouble shooting fallow situations.

It is always beneficial to investigate fallow periods during a simulation before looking at periods containing crops.  There are less issues to think about during fallow periods.  Issues avoided include feedback of crops on water balance (e.g. cover and extraction) and feedback of water balance on crops (e.g. stress).
