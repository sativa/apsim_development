Simulation Malfunction

Simulation Misbehaviour is almost always due to erroneous simulation management logic.  

If you are using APSFront to generate simulation logic then you will need to approach your support person for information regarding the options you are using.

If you are editing your simulation files manually then check the following areas in your logic.

  1. Date Checking - Are the date windows configured correctly in the logic?

  2. Use of manager local variables - Are they being updated and tested properly?  Rerun your simulation with these values reported in the output file and check the results.

  3. Are all your sown crops being harvested?  You may have problems sowing crops if previous crops are not being harvested.

But most importantly, READ THE SUMMARY FILE.  The summary file often contains information regarding the significant events occurring during a simulation.  You may be surprised when you read through the simulation summary.
